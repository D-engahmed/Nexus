## Chapter 1 – Overview

### Purpose

The Future Roadmap module defines the strategic vision and evolution roadmap for the **[Platform Name]** platform. This encompasses the long-term product vision, strategic initiatives, phased roadmap, multi-country expansion, AI-driven capabilities, autonomous logistics, sustainability goals, and continuous improvement practices.

The roadmap provides a clear direction for the platform's evolution over the next 5-10 years. It ensures that the platform remains competitive, innovative, and aligned with market trends and customer needs. This module serves as the strategic guide for product development, investment decisions, and resource allocation.

### Objectives

- Define the long-term product vision
- Establish strategic initiatives and priorities
- Provide a phased roadmap with clear milestones
- Enable multi-country and multi-currency expansion
- Drive AI and automation capabilities
- Support autonomous logistics integration
- Deliver sustainability and ESG goals
- Enable continuous improvement and innovation
